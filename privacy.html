We are the data controller for the personal information collected through this website. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at: castlevale_publishers@tutanota.com

What data we collect and why

When you submit the form on this website, we collect your email address. We collect this for one purpose: to send you a link to your reader resource (the interactive prompts page), and to let you know when new books in this series are published.

We do not collect your name, payment details, location, or any other personal information through this website. Your book purchase is handled entirely by Amazon and is governed by Amazon's privacy policy.

Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

Our legal basis for processing your email address is consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR). By submitting your email address and confirming you agree to receive emails from us, you give us permission to contact you for the purposes described above.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.

How we use your email address

  • To send you a one-time access link to the interactive prompts page
  • To notify you when new books in the AI-Powered Skill to Business series are published
  • Occasionally, to share relevant updates or reader resources related to the series

We will not use your email address for any other purpose, and we will not send you emails from unrelated third parties.

Who processes your data

We use ConvertKit (Kit.com) to manage our email list and send emails. ConvertKit is a US-based company. To comply with UK GDPR requirements for international data transfers, we have a Data Processing Agreement in place with ConvertKit, which includes Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

ConvertKit's privacy policy is available at kit.com/privacy.

No other third party receives your personal data from us.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address on our list for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your email address is removed from our active mailing list. We may retain a suppression record (a record that you have unsubscribed) to ensure we do not contact you again in error.

If you request complete deletion of your data, we will remove all records within 30 days.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can ask us what data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email
  • Right to data portability — you can ask for a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — you can object to us processing your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at castlevale_publishers@tutanota.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies. We do not use analytics tracking, advertising pixels, or any other tracking technology on this site.

Emails we send through ConvertKit may contain a tracking pixel that records whether the email was opened. You can disable this by turning off image loading in your email client.

How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal data, please contact us first at castlevale_publishers@tutanota.com and we will do our best to resolve your concern.

If you remain unhappy, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Telephone: 0303 123 1113

If you are based in the European Union, you may also contact your local data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without giving you notice.
